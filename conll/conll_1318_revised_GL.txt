What is the genetic risk?
Genetic risk refers more to your chance of inheriting a disorder or disease.
People get the specific disease because of genetic changes.
How much a genetic change tells us about your chance of developing a disorder is not always clear.
Suppose your genetic results indicate that you have gene changes associated with an increased risk of heart disease. In that case, it does not mean you will develop heart disease.
The opposite is also true.
Suppose your genetic results show that you do not have changes associated with an increased risk of heart disease. In that case, it is still possible that you will develop heart disease.
However, for some rare diseases, people who have specific gene changes are guaranteed to develop the disease.

To put it in a nutshell, everything has its pros and cons.
So does social media network.
It is not only convenient and beneficial in connecting people and developing the business, but it can also damage people's relationships and interpersonal skills.
